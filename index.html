Interview

What made you pursue web development over film production?

I always liked watching ideas that I had in my head go from conception to execution. This was partially the reason why I was attracted to film production in the first place. Instead of videos though, I enjoyed a greater satisfaction building websites, as it allowed me to connect with users on a more intimate level than film has allowed me to achieve. There are so many ideas that I have in my head begging to be made and I wanted to fufill that drive by pursuing web development.
